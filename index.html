Discover the Flavor of Thailand

Tom Yum Goong is a classic Thai soup renowned for its bold flavors and aromatic spices. This hot and sour soup features a tantalizing blend of lemongrass, galangal, and and kaffir lime leaves, combined with succulent shrimp.

"The key to a deliciuos Tom Yum Goong lies in the balance of flavors - sweet, suor, salty and spicy."-Chef Somchai

Ingredients

  • Lemongrass: 2 Stalks
  • Thai Basil: 1 Cup
  • Kaffir Lime: 3 Leaves
  • Shrimp: 500g

Preparations

  1. Simmer the broth for 10 minutes.
  2. Add lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves.
  3. Stirin shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Season with fish sauce , lime juice, and chili past.
  5. Garnish with Thai Basil and serve hot.

Cooking Tips

  • 1/4cup of fish sauce adds authentic Thai flavor.
  • tablespoon of chili paste gives the soup its signature heat.
